Hiring Insights
Last week had a great discussion with Kathryne at Talentful (our recruitment partner).
Kathryne talked through some stats and insights to help me understand time to hire (TTH) and time to fill (TTF) for engineering roles (https://resources.workable.com/tutorial/faq-time-to-fill-hire - to add as shortened link please).
On average it takes roughly 65 calendar days from opening a role to making an offer (TTF) - note, this does not include notice periods. The long TTF shows the clear need to build a bench for future work and backfill recent leavers.

Together we also discussed the importance of diversity in engineering, Kathryne has taken an action to dig out some data on women in leadership roles to help us set expectations and become more strategic about how we hire e.g. What is the likely hood of hiring female leadership in office-based roles? Do we stand a better chance of hiring more female leads if we cast a wider net by opening up to remote roles outside of London and Bristol?
